WELCOME TO KRISHI SATHI

Smart Agriculture Project:"KRISHI SATHI"

Technology has played the very vital role in bringing the whole world into the modern era.It has covered almost all the field that are familiar to us.To bring the change in farming process in the agricultural coutries this very Smart Agricultural Project titled as Krishi Sathi has been introduced.With the help of the hardware module contained with arduino uno AT MEGA 328 implanted on the soil,the pH value,moisturity content,humidity and temperature of the soil is measured out and stored in my SQL Database through wifi bee and with the help of the database,the suitable crops according to the favorable parameters are suggested to the farmers with the motive to give rise to the cultivation of suitable crops.This project accounts with internet of thing(IOT).
